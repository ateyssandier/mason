Residence Rules

Students can expect to share a room with one other camper and have a Resident Advisor close by on the same floor.

The RA (Resident Advisor) will check each room at 11. Supervisors are there to make sure all the campers are safe, accounted for and abiding by the camp rules.

  1. Campers will be responsible for any damage to the room. All campers will be assigned a roommate.
  2. Valuables such as money, watches, and jewelry should be properly safeguarded.
  3. Athletic activities are prohibited in the residence halls. Running, playing, throwing, and similar activities are to be done outside the buildings.
  4. The following are not permitted on campus or in the residence halls: fireworks or explosives of any kind, firearms, pets, illegal drugs, alcoholic beverages, and any other items that could cause bodily harm or damage. Smoking is also prohibited in all buildings on campus. Any form of fire, incense, or candles is also prohibited.
  5. Each camp member is to be properly dressed.
  6. All camp members are expected to respect the rights and property of others. Loud noise and similar intrusive behavior are prohibited.
  7. Other basic rules of conduct will be presented during orientation. Failure to observe these requests could result in early dismissal from the camp with no refund of tuition and fees.
  8. There will be counselors in each floor to assist you.
  9. Students must be in rooms by 10:30 each night. Resident Advisor will do a room check before lights out at 11.

Gym rules

  1. Campers are expected to respect all CBA coaches, equipment and gym facilities.
  2. Coaches are here to help teach and train you on how to improve in the game of basketball. Respect them by listening to them and following their instruction.
  3. Take care of the equipment. Basketballs, medicine balls, fitness ladders etc are all tools used to help teach you the game of basketball. Use as instructed.
  4. Take care of gym facilities. Wear appropriate gym sneakers. Dark soled running shoes, turf shoes, or spiked shoes that can mark up the gym floor are not permitted. Wear appropriate shorts, t-shirts or tank tops specific to basketball.
  5. CBA will provide healthy snacks and drinks at the gym every day. Please only eat and drink in designated areas.
  6. Respect other campers. Fighting, stealing, horseplay is not permitted.

Classroom rules

  1. Students are expected to be at class on time, with proper materials
  2. Students my respect the teacher at all times by paying attention and not speaking out of turn.
  3. Students must always do their own work.

Excursion rules

  1. Students must ALWAYS stay with the guide and/or chaperones.
  2. If free time is given, students must return to the meeting point at or before the time given.
  3. Students must check in with chaperone before boarding the bus
  4. Students must not under any circumstances leave with or get into a car with strangers
  5. Students must abide by all laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts and of the United States.
