Boston Area

The Boston area has lots to offer. Below are links to some out its most interesting attractions and descriptions of excursions and events offered at CBA!

  • Boston Freedom Trail Tour:
    The Freedom Trail is always on the top of a Boston first timer’s list. This 2 plus mile walk through the heart of Boston, passes by over 16 historically significant locations including the State House, Faneuil Hall, and the Old North Church.
  • Shopping on Newbury St.
    Eight blocks filled with salons, boutiques, and fabulous dining. Boston's Newbury Street has something for everyone including Armani, Nike, and Apple. http://www.newbury-st.com
  • Shopping at “the Coop”:
    The official book and clothing store of Harvard University. Load up on new gear for yourself and great presents to take home to family and friends.
  • College Visits:
    Visit some of Boston’s many great Universities including Harvard, MIT, Boston College, Boston University, Tufts, and many more!
  • Chinatown
  • Boston Museum of Science:
    Famous for its location, a plot of land spanning the Charles River as well as its over 700 interactive exhibits, the Museum offers live presentations, a planetarium and the the only domed IMAX screen in New England.
  • Basketball Hall of Fame:
    The place where all of the best basketball players and coaches have been enshrined, the Basketball Hall of Fame Museum!
