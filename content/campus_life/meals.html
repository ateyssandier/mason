Meals

Breakfast

A continental breakfast will be provided each morning at the Camp Hotel which will include various cereals, pastries, fruit, juice, tea and coffee.

Lunch

A hot lunch will be provided on site each day catered from a local Cambridge area restaurant.

Dinner

Students are invited to eat out with their friends in one of many wonderful places in close proximity to the hotel. An inclusive Grand Feast in Chinatown will take place on one evening during the week.
