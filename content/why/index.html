The Cambridge Basketball Academy Experience

The Cambridge Basketball Academy is a unique, high end, basketball instruction academy in the heart of Cambridge, Massachusetts. Our camp sessions are placed during popular school break periods such as Holiday, Winter, Spring and Summer, which makes it easy for students to attend and gives them something meaningful to do during their down time. We take advantage of our prime location within the educational and research capital of the world by providing top basketball instruction while taking time to visit the area's top universities, shopping districts, and tons of great basketball related activities.

A limited number of students are admitted each year to immerse themselves in basketball before they begin their rigorous course work at their American high school of choice. By welcoming the students to join us in Cambridge, Massachusetts we offer an intense, yet enjoyable, week of elite basketball instruction all while helping the student to become more comfortable listening and speaking in English. Coach Holloway will use his expertise to teach the students how to play the game the right way and what high school coaches in the United States look for in potential team members.

During our Summer Session, campers will meet with a personal student mentor from top Boston Universities such as Harvard and MIT. This one-to-one meeting will provide the student will invaluable insight into the work ethics and habits of their mentors.
