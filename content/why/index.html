Coach Neil Holloway

Coach Neil Holloway has coached in several schools throughput the US. Currently he is the Head Basketball Coach at the International School of Boston.

It is during this time that he has noticed the influx of international players looking to transition their way into basketball in the US. With his knowledge of the game of basketball combined with his understanding of private school leagues, he is in a unique position to help guide international players attending prestigious New England private schools.

Along with his assistants, Coach Holloway will instruct campers on the fundamental skills needed to become successful players. He focuses on drills that improve footwork, shooting, defense and offensive concepts. He teaches an American style of Basketball that emphasizes a fast break, uptempo game.

Whether you are just beginning playing basketball or are an experienced player looking to improve your skills Coach Holloway will give you the tools to help you succeed on the court
