The Cambridge Basketball Academy Experience

The Cambridge Basketball Academy offers high-end basketball instruction located in the heart of the “Education and Research Capital of the World,” Cambridge, Massachusetts. Cambridge and Boston are known worldwide for the high concentration of top Colleges and Universities, high-tech companies, and renowned Hospitals.

By joining us at the Academy, you will be immersed in Basketball and English in the heart of this great area. You will have the opportunity to explore some of local Universities and make new friends along the way. Coach Neil Holloway will teach you the skills and mentality needed to compete for a roster spot on your local high school team next season.

We offer two different camps, the School Break Camp which takes place over Holiday, Winter, and Spring breaks and the Summer Camp which takes place in August. The School Break Camps are designed to get students out of homestays and dorms and into a new city for exploration, sights, and of course, plenty of basketball! The Summer Camp in August offers all of these things, plus the opportunity to spend time with a local area College Student from schools such as Harvard or MIT. This one-on-one time is a great opportunity to learn the study habits and work ethics of some of the area’s most successful students!

Space is limited so please enroll today!
