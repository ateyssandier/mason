Coach Neil Holloway

Coach Neil Holloway has coached in several schools throughout the US. Currently, he is the Head Basketball Coach at the International School of Boston.

His position at the International School has given him a unique ability to recognize the needs of international players looking to transition their way into basketball in the US. Armed with these skills, students will enhance their ability to make their respective High School teams and ultimately bolster their resumes when applying to college.

Along with his assistants, Coach Holloway will instruct campers on the fundamental skills needed to become successful players. He focuses on drills that improve footwork, shooting, defense and offensive concepts. He teaches an American style of Basketball that emphasizes a fast break, uptempo game.

Whether you are just beginning playing basketball or are an experienced player looking to improve your skills Coach Holloway will give you the tools to help you succeed on the court
