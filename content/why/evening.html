Evening Mentor Program

The location of the Cambridge Basketball Academy is the key reason why we can offer our one-of-a-kind Evening Mentor program. Your child will have the extremely unique opportunity to spend time getting to know a current student from a top Boston area university such as Harvard or MIT. The informal dinner meetings will give them the chance to find out the secrets, habits, and attitude that can help one be admitted to a top US University after High School. They have done it, and they will tell your child how! This feature can not be found in any other basketball camp in the world.
