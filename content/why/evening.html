Evening Mentor Program (Summer session only)

The location of the Cambridge Basketball Academy is the key reason why we can offer our one-of-a-kind Evening Mentor program.

You will have the unique opportunity to spend time getting to know a current student from a top Boston area university such as Harvard or MIT.

These one-on-one dinner meetings will give you the chance to find out the habits, attitudes and work ethic it takes to be successful in your upcoming entrance into higher education.
