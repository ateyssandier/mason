School Break Camps

School Break Camp at CBA is all about getting out of your home stay or dorm and coming to the Boston area for Basketball and Sightseeing. Not only will you get all the great basketball instruction that the summer camp offers, you will also be invited to join us on multiple day/night excursions throughout the area including;



  • All Camp Instruction
  • Harvard University Tour
  • Basketball Hall of Fame
  • Feast in Chinatown
  • Boston Celtics Game (dependent on availability)
  • Shopping on Newbury Street (Boston's best Shopping District)
  • CBA T-shirts
  • Hotel Accommodations for 5 Nights (Sunday-Friday) with Wireless Internet
  • Breakfast and Lunch.
