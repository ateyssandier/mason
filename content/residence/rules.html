Rules

General Camp Rules 1. Students must be ready on time for morning pickup 2. Students must have proper basketball attire on for each Camp session (sneakers, shorts, CBA jersey) 3. Students will be expected to participate fully in all basketball related activities 4. Students will respect coaches by listening to all instruction 5. Students are expected to play fairly and respect other players. Fighting or taunting will not be tolerated. 6. Although not exclusively, students should attempt to communicate in English as best as they can. 7. Have fun!
