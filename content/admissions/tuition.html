Tuition

School Break Camp at CBA is all about getting out of your home stay or dorm and coming to the Boston area for basketball and sightseeing.   • All Camp Instruction
  • Harvard University Tour
  • Basketball Hall of Fame

  • Shopping on Newbury Street (Boston's best Shopping District)
  • CBA T-shirt
  • Hotel Accommodations for 5 Nights (Sunday-Friday) with Wireless Internet
  • Breakfast and Lunch.

All this is included in a one time tuition payment $3,450
