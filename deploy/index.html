  • What is CBA

    Cambridge Basketball Academy is a summer basketball camp in Boston and Cambridge. We also offer a school break basketball camp in the Boston/Cambridge area. The Cambridge Basketball Academy offers high quality basketball instruction and English language training combined with a culturally immersive and comprehensive tour of Boston area Educational Institutions and historical sites. Several Camp sessions are typically offered each summer as well as during holiday school breaks. Taking advantage of our prime location within the “educational and research capital of the world,” international campers are paired with local basketball players their own age to create a camp experience with no equal!

  • Who Can Attend

    Cambridge Basketball Academy from Cambridge Basketball Academy on Vimeo.

    CBA is offered to young men of all skill levels from the ages of 8-18. Family members are welcome accompany student to Boston. CBA will arrange flight, hotel arrangements (same as students) and suggest activities.
